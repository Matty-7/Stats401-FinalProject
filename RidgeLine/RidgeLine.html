The Ridge Line Plot explores the salary distribution across different education levels in the U.S., using data from Glassdoor. One interactive feature allows users to adjust the kernel value of the Kernel Density Estimation (KDE) via a slider, affecting the shape of the density curves. Another slider enables users to tweak the smoothing parameter, allowing for finer or coarser granularity in the density estimation.
