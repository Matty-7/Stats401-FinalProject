The Bubble Map visualizes the geographic distribution of job opportunities in the U.S., with each bubble's size proportional to the number of jobs available in that city. Users can filter the displayed bubbles by the number of job opportunities in each city, choosing from categories like "All," "0-50 jobs," "50-150 jobs," and "150+ jobs." A hoverboard feature activates when users hover over a bubble, providing detailed job count and city information for that specific location.
