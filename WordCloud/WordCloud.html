The Word Cloud plot visually represents the most frequently occurring job titles in the U.S. job market, sourced from LinkedIn's dataset. A filter allows users to sort the cloud based on four metrics: frequency, maximum salary, median salary, and minimum salary. Upon making a selection, a hoverboard feature activates to display specific details about each job title, enriching the user's interactive experience.
