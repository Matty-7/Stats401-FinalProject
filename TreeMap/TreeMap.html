Tree Map Description: This is a visualization of companies, their employee count, and the industries they belong to. Each cell represents a company, and each greater colored rectangle represents industry. Hover your mouse above the cells to see the company name, the corresponding industry, and the employee number. Use the filter for a detailed view of each industry.
